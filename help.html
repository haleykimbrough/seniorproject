Description

Pain Link is a website that helps patients identify factors that can trigger spikes in pain. You can enter a variety of information and Pain Link will show your pain levels from each day on a chart. It will also store this data and display the factors that are often present with higher than normal pain levels. When data is present multiple times during spikes in pain it can be an indicator that that specific factor might be causing your pain to be worse.

Instructions

To input data:
Make your way to the home page by clicking back or the home button. Once on the home page, click the calendar button and it will take you to a page with a calendar and you can choose which day you'd like to enter data or edit previously entered data. For both entering and editing data, you just click the drop down button and choose the option you want to be entered. Once you are finished entering in the data, you can click on submit, the data will be saved, and you will be redirected back to the main page where you can see the new data displayed on the chart or in the list of possible pain triggers.

To delete your account:
Click on the delete account button. It will redirect you to another page where you will confirm that you want your account deleted.

Helpful Tips

How to create a shortcut to the website from your home page on iPhone:
Click the button on the bottom center with a rounded square and an up arrow. Scroll to and click where it says "Add to Home Screen." You can change the name or keep it and then click "Add" in the top right corner. You shoudl see it added to your home screen and from there, you can move the shortcut around the home screen.

How to set daily reminders with an iPhone:
Open up your Calendar app and click on today's date. Click the plus sign in the top right corner and you'll be able to enter in the "title" of your reminder (that is what it will say every day when it gives you the reminder). Where it says "Starts" you can click and choose the time you want to be reminded. Click "Repeat" and choose "Every Day." Further down, click "Alert" and choose if you want to be reminded at the time of the event or a certain amount of time before the event. Lastly, click "Add" in the top right corner and you're done!
